Zoonar Logistics

Zoonar Logistics provides full-service logistics management for businesses that demand control, efficiency and scalability. We act as your strategic logistics partner — managing operations, optimizing supply chains and executing transport worldwide.

Supply Chain Management

We design, optimize and manage complete supply chains from end to end. Our approach focuses on creating efficient, scalable and cost-effective logistics structures tailored to your business.

Our responsibilities include supplier coordination, inventory planning, demand forecasting, process optimization and continuous performance monitoring. We identify inefficiencies and implement strategic improvements that reduce operational costs while increasing delivery reliability.

From sourcing to final delivery, we ensure your entire supply chain operates as one streamlined system.

Global Transport (Sea, Air & Road)

We manage international transport across all major modalities — sea freight, air freight and road transport — ensuring your goods move efficiently across global markets.

Our services include route planning, carrier selection, rate optimization, shipment tracking and full coordination of logistics partners. We adapt transport strategies based on urgency, cost and reliability.

Whether it’s full container loads, urgent air shipments or cross-border trucking, we ensure seamless execution from origin to destination.

Logistics Control Tower

Our Logistics Control Tower acts as the central hub of your entire logistics operation. We provide real-time visibility, coordination and control over all supply chain activities.

We manage communication between suppliers, carriers, warehouses and customers, ensuring every part of the operation is aligned. Through data monitoring and proactive decision-making, we prevent delays and optimize performance.

This centralized approach allows you to focus on growth while we manage the complexity behind the scenes.

Additional Services

In addition to our core logistics services, we support your operations with a range of essential activities that ensure smooth execution across your supply chain.

We handle documentation and customs processes, ensuring compliance with international regulations and minimizing delays at borders.

Our warehousing and storage solutions provide flexible capacity and secure handling of goods, while our distribution services ensure efficient delivery to your customers.

Together, these services create a complete logistics solution tailored to your business needs.
